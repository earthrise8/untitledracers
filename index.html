What Is H2GP?

Competition info pulled from H2GP/STEP pages

Global Program

Student Hydrogen Racing

H2GP describes itself as a global racing series where students design, build, and race hydrogen-powered cars through hands-on STEM learning.

Age Pathway

XPR, Sprint, Pro

Program bands include XPR (ages 7-9), Sprint (ages 10-13), and Pro (ages 13-17), with Pro focused on advanced engineering and international competition.

Race Format

Endurance + Strategy

Official H2GP Pro racing materials reference a 4-hour race format, with teams managing speed, energy, and pit decisions.

Step 1: Local and regional qualifiers
Step 2: National-level race events
Step 3: World Final week with racing, workshops, and cultural exchange

World Final context: STEP Foundation event pages describe top student teams and highlight week-long finals that combine racing with technology workshops, industry exposure, and international collaboration.
